### Exercice 7
- *French:* Calculez la limite en $$0$$ de $$f(x) = \frac{1 - \cos x \cdot \cos 2x \cdot \cos 3x}{x^2}$$.
- *English:* Compute the limit at $$0$$ of $$f(x) = \frac{1 - \cos x \cdot \cos 2x \cdot \cos 3x}{x^2}$$.

#### Answers
- *Correct:* $$7$$.
- *False:* $$[\lbrace \frac{1}{2}, 0, 8 \rbrace]$$.
